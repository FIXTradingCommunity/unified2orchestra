### Codeset LocateReqdCodeSet type Boolean (114)

Indicates whether the broker is to locate the stock in conjunction with a short sell order.

| Name | Value | Id     | Sort | Synopsis                                                   |
|------|-------|--------|------|------------------------------------------------------------|
| No   | N     | 114001 | 1    | Indicates the broker is not required to locate             |
| Yes  | Y     | 114002 | 2    | Indicates the broker is responsible for locating the stock |
